06 — Business

Behind the
music.

Producing is half of it. The other half is the business of making things happen — from a #1 record rollout to an AI product launch at CES.

  1. Oct 2023 — Present Los Angeles

    Founder & Producer

    Sidewalk Studios

    • Founded and operate an independent music production studio — artist relationships, project timelines, and studio operations, end to end.
    • Built a royalty-share model that waives upfront fees for a share of downstream distribution — opening real production to artists who can’t front the cost.
  2. Oct — Dec 2025 Los Angeles

    Project Manager Contract

    FYI RAiDiO founded by will.i.am

    • Ran cross-functional execution of an AI-integrated radio product roadmap — engineering, compliance, data capture, marketing, and sales — toward a CES 2026 launch.
    • Owned regulatory and compliance readiness across product-development milestones.
  3. Sep 2022 — Oct 2023 Los Angeles

    Manager & Executive Assistant

    Hallwood Media for will.i.am

    • Day-to-day for will.i.am — releases, broadcasts, live performances, budgets, clearances, and scheduling in a high-volume, detail-critical room.
    • Shipped “THE FORMULA,” “MIND YOUR BUSINESS,” and “LET’S GO” (ft. Lil Wayne, Britney Spears, David Guetta, J Balvin) — “Mind Your Business” hit #1 on Billboard’s Dance/Electronic Digital Song Sales chart.
    • Coordinated the inaugural Formula 1 Artist-in-Residency; built brand partnerships with Fender, Moog, Eventide, PreSonus, and Antares.
  4. Nov 2021 — Aug 2022 Los Angeles

    Studio Assistant for Jason Hill

    Department of Recording & Power

    • Assisted development, production, mixing, and integration of film scores for Netflix, Hulu, and HBO, alongside songs for independent artists.
    • Hands-on across synthesizers, guitars, keys, strings, and a wide orchestra of instruments — plus preamps, compressors, EQs, and outboard gear.

08 — Science

Before the
music.

Before the studio, there was a microscope. A mycology degree from Cornell, summers in research labs, and a question that never quite closed — what fungi can do for the rest of us.

  1. 2017 — 2021 Ithaca, NY

    B.S. Biological Sciences Mycology

    Cornell University

    • Summa Cum Laude · 3.96 GPA · Dean’s List, 2017–2021.
    • NCAA Division I soccer · Cornell Mushroom Club.
  2. May — Jul 2019 Pullman, WA

    Undergraduate Researcher

    Sheppard Lab Washington State University

    • Assisted a Paul Stamets–designed study testing polypore mushroom mycelia extracts to reduce viral loads in honeybees.
    • Ran field prep, cage testing, RNA extraction, and cDNA synthesis and analysis — direct exposure to fungal-compound bioactivity.
  3. Jan — May 2019 Ithaca, NY

    Undergraduate Researcher

    Hodge Lab Cornell University

    • Contributed to the discovery of a novel virulence pathway of the fungal pathogen Paecilomyces niveus on post-harvest grapes.
    • Built proficiency in DNA extraction, spore counting, culture creation, inoculation, and mycelial infection microscopy.

Ongoing independent study — the literature of psilocybin-assisted therapy: Griffiths, Carhart-Harris, Nutt, Stamets, Dosenbach & Siegel, and Doblin / MAPS.
